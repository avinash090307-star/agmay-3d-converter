How It Works

The most advanced AI pipeline, from image to production-ready 3D model.

01

Upload Image

Drag & drop any image of an object, character, or product. PNG, JPG, or WebP supported.

02

Multi-View Diffusion

Our Unique3D-style model generates 4 orthographic views with normal maps from your single image.

03

ISOMER Mesh Gen

Our advanced mesh reconstruction algorithm deforms a template mesh to fit the multi-view priors with millions of faces.

04

PBR Texturing + Export

UV-unwrapped textures with PBR materials (albedo, roughness, metallic, normal). Export to GLB, OBJ, FBX, or PLY.
